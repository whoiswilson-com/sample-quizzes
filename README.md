# Who Is Wilson? — Sample Study Quizzes

Community-contributed sample study questions for use with [whoiswilson.app](https://whoiswilson.app).

> **These are example study questions created by the community.** They are not sourced from, affiliated with, or endorsed by any certification body (Microsoft, Amazon, CompTIA, etc.). They exist to help you get started — expand on them, rewrite them in your own words, and create your own.

## What is this?

[Who Is Wilson?](https://whoiswilson.com) is a free exam prep tool where you write your own quiz questions in a simple text format, then test yourself as often as you like. Writing questions in your own words is one of the most effective ways to study.

This repository provides **starter quizzes** so you don't begin with a blank page. Pick a topic, download the file, and upload it to your account. Then make it yours — edit questions, add new ones, and write explanations that make sense to you.

## Browse by topic

## How to use

1. Browse the folders above and find a quiz relevant to your studies
2. Download the `.md` file (click the file, then click the **Raw** button and save)
3. Log in to [whoiswilson.app](https://whoiswilson.app)
4. Upload the file — or open the **Quiz Editor** and paste the content in
5. Edit the questions, add your own, and start testing
